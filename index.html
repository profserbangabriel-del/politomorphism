λ Empirical Calibration: The Decay Parameter Is Not a Constant

Using Google Trends data for all 19 validated symbol-periods, λ was computed empirically from the ratio of average to peak search intensity. Result: λ ranges from 2.00 (Sunflower Movement — civic mobilization) to 104.66 (Charlie Hebdo — extreme flash viral) — a 52-fold variation. The SRM formula is unchanged; λ is now measured, not assumed.
